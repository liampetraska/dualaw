About Dua & Associates, PLLC

We Care About You!

Dua & Associates, PLLC was established in 2001 and has been in the Greater Lansing area ever since. We are familiar with the area and frequently appear the courts of Ingham, Clinton, Eaton Counties. Our practice covers all of mid and southeastern Michigan. As a smaller firm, we are here to assist with your legal needs, but more importantly, we are here to counsel. Our firm does not have any billable hour requirements for its attorneys. We understand how costly and time consuming legal matters can get and we do our best to keep your costs to a minimum. We would rather have you as a life-long, satisfied, repeat client, rather than a one time deal.
